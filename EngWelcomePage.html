Making friends can be a challenge!

Finding someone with similar interests, thoughts and behaviour, or even someone who identifies with your feelings, is not an easy task for anyone. In many cases, people end up feeling lonely and unacknowledged.

We propose to create an app which will allow users to meet new people based on a more extensive selection of criteria, including those less accepted and acknowledged. Once a profile is created, each user may search for a person to talk to based on their criteria, or find a community of which they may partake in.
