Interact

Making friends can be a challenge.

Finding someone with similar interests, thoughts and behaviour, or even someone who identify with your feelings, is not an easy task for everyone. In many cases, people end up feeling lonely and not acknowledged.

For these situations, we propose to make it easier for people to meet others but also to find some support for their daily struggles, by giving them access to some special features, non existent in the current social media apps.
